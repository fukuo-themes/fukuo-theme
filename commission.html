What is custom theme (or custom page)?

First of all, thanks for your interest! Custom theme is a personalized theme only for you. It means that I will be making a theme based on your idea. I will do the work after receiving your concept — well, you create a sketch of the theme on how you want it to look. Usually, you provide the details for me (what type of font you'd like to include on the theme, the color schemes, how many options and many more). The following are the processes in a simpler way:

You give me the concept

We work on our theme

You receive the theme

*The process is not as simple as this but you get a gist.
I also accept non-related Tumblr requests such as Landing Page, Interactive Page and Portfolio Website (hosted on Github, Gitlab, and/or Netlify), if you're interested!

How much do you charge?

I have set the price depending on the complexity. The pricing as follows:
  • Custom theme starting from $25
  • Custom page starting from $10

The price will be vary depending on your complexity. If the jQuery/Javascript is involved on your theme or pages, I will charge more. But! If you give me a sketch of your design (and of course, your detailed explanation), I will offer a discount for you.

Oh, okay. What can you code?

HTML, CSS, and jQuery/JavaScript (vanilla or Vue.js)! I will let you know straight away if your request is beyond my capability

How is the procedure?

  1. After submitting the commission form, I will send you an email discussing our "product"
  2. If the design/mockup document is ready to be executed, I will begin coding and show my in-progress available to you for viewing so you can suggest any additional changes (sometimes, I will ask you to pay the base price first but most of my previous clients prefer to pay after the theme is done so...)
  3. Once our product is done, I will ask you to pay the theme before sending the code
  4. The code will be given and I will do the maintenance and provide support to you

How long it will take you to code the theme?

Honestly, it depends on my schedule. I currently have a full-time job so it will probably take more time than I used to. I usually give an approximate estimate on the form below for when I can finish your theme. For rather a simpler theme, I will most likely be able to finish it in two weeks.

Please keep in mind that...

  • For your information, I only accept payment via Paypal
  • I will never publish the theme you've requested so it's totally yours. But please remember, don't use the customized theme for multiply blog!
  • I will not reproduce someone's layout for you.
  • Please remember, if you're interested in commissioning me, give me the details.
    A sketch/design of you is also very helpful (If you have any)! So I can work it based on how you want it to look.
  • Do not redistribute and claim as your own. Respect my time and effort working on this project. It may takes some time so be patient!
